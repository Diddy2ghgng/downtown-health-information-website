Common Illnesses We Research

Cancer ▼

Causes: Genetic mutations, lifestyle factors.

Symptoms: Fatigue, weight loss, lumps.

Treatment: Chemotherapy, radiation, surgery.

Learn more about Cancer

Hypertension ▼

Causes: High salt intake, stress, obesity.

Symptoms: Headaches, dizziness, blurred vision.

Treatment: Medication, lifestyle changes.

Learn more about Hypertension

Diabetes ▼

Causes: Insulin resistance, genetics.

Symptoms: Excessive thirst, fatigue, slow healing wounds.

Treatment: Insulin therapy, diet control.

Learn more about Diabetes

HIV/AIDS ▼

Causes: Virus transmission via bodily fluids.

Symptoms: Weak immune system, fever, night sweats.

Treatment: Antiretroviral therapy (ART).

Learn more about HIV/AIDS
